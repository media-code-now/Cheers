L’Chaim Cocktail Bar is a unique pop-up bartending service based in Las Vegas, NV. Having an on-the-go bar at your event is not only about quenching your thirst, it is about the experience you and your guests receive. We always pay full attention to the tiniest detail, making sure each drink is mixed to precision and served with pleasure to your party.

With L’Chaim, not only will you get custom cocktails designed to your liking, you will host a memorable event your guests won’t stop talking about. With several add-ons to choose from, we have the luxury of providing exactly what you need for your perfect event and making your wildest drinks come true! So what are you waiting for?
